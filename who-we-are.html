About Us

About Johnny Davis
Global Missions

Johnny Davis Global Missions is a non-profit organization founded on the belief that a little help can go a long way. Our mission is to help those in need, transforming lives and empowering communities for the Kingdom of God.

From food and medical care to education and disaster relief, our organization provides the necessary resources and support that every child, adult, and community deserves. No border is too far, no need too great — because love has no limits.

What We Do

Our Core Mission Focus

Every program we run is rooted in compassion and driven by the urgent needs of communities across the Philippines.

🍚

Feeding Programs

Providing nutritious meals to children and families facing hunger across underserved communities.

🏥

Medical Assistance

Delivering essential healthcare, medicines, and medical missions to those who cannot afford care.

🎓

Education Support

Equipping children and youth with learning resources, school supplies, and scholarship opportunities.

🌏

Disaster Relief

Responding swiftly to typhoons, earthquakes, and floods with food, water, and emergency shelter.
